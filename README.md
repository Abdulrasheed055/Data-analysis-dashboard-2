# Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel | Power Query | Pivot Tables)

An interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product demand, and delivery efficiency. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights using **Power Query**, **Pivot Tables**, **Pivot Charts**, and **Slicers**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Businesses generate thousands of sales transactions every year, making it difficult to manually identify trends and monitor performance.

This project provides an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor sales performance
- Analyze customer spending
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare revenue across occasions
- Track monthly and hourly sales trends
- Evaluate delivery efficiency
- Explore regional sales performance

---

##  Business Objective

Develop an interactive dashboard that helps decision-makers answer key business questions through data visualization and KPI tracking.

# 📈 Key Insights

###  Revenue Performance

- Generated **£3.52M** in total revenue from **1,000 orders**.
- Average customer spending reached **£3,520.98**, indicating relatively high-value transactions.

---

###  Product Popularity by Occasion

- **Anniversary** generated the highest sales revenue.
- **Raksha Bandhan** and **Holi** also performed strongly.
- **Diwali** and **Valentine's Day** generated comparatively lower revenue.

**Business Insight**

Seasonal celebrations significantly influence customer purchasing behavior. Marketing campaigns should prioritize high-performing occasions while introducing promotional strategies for lower-performing events.

---

###  Revenue by Product Category

Highest revenue categories include:

- Colors
- Soft Toys
- Sweets

Lower-performing categories include:

- Plants
- Mugs
- Raksha Bandhan products

**Business Insight**

High-performing categories should receive increased inventory investment and marketing support, while low-performing categories require pricing or promotional review.

---

###  Monthly Revenue Trend

Revenue fluctuates considerably throughout the year.

Peak sales occurred during:

- February
- August
- November

Lower sales were recorded during:

- January
- April
- September

**Business Insight**

Sales are seasonal. Inventory planning and marketing campaigns should be aligned with anticipated demand.

---

###  Revenue by Order Time

Customer purchases increase during later business hours, with noticeable fluctuations throughout the day.

**Business Insight**

Peak ordering periods provide opportunities for targeted promotions and optimized staffing.

---

###  Top Products by Revenue

The highest revenue-generating products include:

- Magnum Set
- Quia Gift
- Dolores Gift
- Harum Pack
- Deserunt Box

**Business Insight**

These products consistently drive revenue and should remain a priority for inventory planning and promotional campaigns.

---

###  Top Cities by Orders

Cities with the highest order volumes include:

- Imphal
- Dhanbad
- Kavali

Some cities show significantly lower demand.

**Business Insight**

Expand marketing efforts in high-performing cities while implementing localized campaigns to improve sales in underperforming regions.

---

###  Delivery Performance

Average order-to-delivery time is **5.53 hours**.

**Business Insight**

Maintaining a delivery time below six hours supports customer satisfaction and improves the likelihood of repeat purchases.

---

#  Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Launch targeted campaigns before peak sales seasons.
- Improve marketing for lower-performing occasions.
- Expand operations in high-demand cities.
- Optimize staffing during peak order hours.
- Continue monitoring delivery performance to maintain service quality.
